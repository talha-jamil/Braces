FAQ

What is Braces?

  • Braces was started so that every student can have that opportunity to learn from his/her classmates and to impact the way students learn and the way instructors teach.Braces is designed to connect students and instructors so every student can get help when needed — even at 2AM. We sincerely hope Braces enhances your experience as a student and as an Instructor.

What is the Criteria to Register in this Student Communication Portal ?

  • Student must have valid university email in order for him to register in Student Communication Portal

What will I find in the Student Communication Portal?

  • Add, Drop for classes
  • Contact the professor with your questions
  • Post question where students can help you to solve the problem
