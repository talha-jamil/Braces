About us


Braces was started so that every student can have that
opportunity to learn from his/her classmates and to impact
the way students learn and the way instructors teach.Braces
is designed to connect students and instructors so every
student can get help when needed — even at 2AM. We sincerely
hope Braces enhances your experience as a student and as an Instructor.
